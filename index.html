About Us

TechXplore VJIT is the premier student-run technology club at Vidya Jyothi Institute of Technology (VJIT). Our mission is to provide students with hands-on learning experiences and industry collaborations in the fields of software testing, ethical hacking, automation, and much more. Through partnerships with leading companies and expert-led workshops, we aim to bridge the gap between classroom knowledge and real-world applications.
